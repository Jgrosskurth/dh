About the Drunken Hitmen

Est. 2001

The Drunken Hitmen have been swinging bats and building brotherhood since 2001. What started as a group of friends looking for some Sunday morning fun has grown into one of the most respected and competitive men's slowpitch softball teams in the Legacy Softball League.

Over 25 years later, the core values remain the same: compete hard, have fun, and always show up for your brothers on the diamond. We've seen players come and go, celebrated championships, inducted Hall of Famers, and created memories that last a lifetime.

Every Sunday morning at 8:30 AM, rain or shine, the Hitmen take the field ready to compete. It's more than a game to us. It's family, tradition, and a quarter-century of doing what we love.
